I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Adobe. I completed my master's degree in Electrical Engineering at the Institute Intelligence and Data (IID) of Laval University, under the supervision of Jean-François Lalonde and Yannick Hold-Geoffroy. My expertise is in 3D computer vision, multimodal generative models, and high-performance distributed systems.

During my masters, I had the opportunity of doing two internships with Adobe, one with the Adobe Research team and one with the 3D & Immersive team in San Francisco. Before that, I completed my bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics at Laval University.
