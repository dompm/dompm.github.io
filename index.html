I am a master’s student in Electrical Engineering at the Institute Intelligence and Data (IID) of Laval University, under the supervision of Jean-François Lalonde and Yannick Hold-Geoffroy. My research interests are in the field of computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on scene understanding and computational photography.

During my masters, I had the opportunity to do two internships with Adobe, one with the Adobe Research team (remote), and one in the 3D & Immersive team (San Francisco, CA). Before that, I completed my bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics at Laval University in 2021, during which I did internships with the Medical Physics Research Group and the Computer Vision and Systems Laboratory.
